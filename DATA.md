# Synthetic Dataset

All data in `/data` is fictional and generated for demonstration. No real people,
companies, or transactions are represented.

It models realistic private-markets scenarios: investors hold allocations in deals
(SPVs), one company can raise multiple rounds, and the platform tracks commitments,
capital calls, fees, valuations, distributions, and account statements across four
currencies (USD/GBP/EUR/AED).

Tables: investors (112), portfolio_companies (16), deals (21), allocations (550),
valuations (55), capital_calls (655), fees (1,401), distributions (34),
statement_lines (1,390), fx_rates (4). Report date: 2026-06-25.
